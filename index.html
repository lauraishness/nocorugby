New to Rugby?

Learn Rugby. Play Rugby.

Ready to learn one of the most physically demanding sports out there? Our team regularly recruits new players of all skill levels to help us compete in Eastern Rockies DIII matches. Our experienced coaches are committed to raising up new players to play well, but also to mitigate the risk of injury. You'll learn how to play hard but also to play smart.

Join us at practices to find out what this great sport is all about. Bring cleats and a mouth guard. In the mean time, explore the links below to learn more about the game.
